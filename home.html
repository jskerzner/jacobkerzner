About Me

 Jacob Kerzner is a music director, singer and composer from Western Massachusetts with a B.A. in Music Composition and Voice Performance from Ithaca College. He has worked for theatre companies such as Transcendence Theatre Company, Barrington Stage Company, and the Muny in St. Louis and has performed at venues such as Lincoln Center, Boston Symphony Hall and Ozawa Hall at Tanglewood. His original compositions have also been performed by ensembles such as the Cayuga Vocal Ensemble, Ithaca College Chorus, and several local elementary and middle schools. He is currently pursuing his M.A. in Musical Theatre (Music Directing) at the Royal Conservatoire of Scotland where he will work at the Edinburgh next summer.

What have I been doing?

Read about where I've been and where I'm heading.

Fall 2018 - Fall 2019

Royal Conservatoire of Scotland

I will be heading to Glasgow in late September to begin studying towards my M.A. in Musical Theatre (Music Directing) at RCS, which culminates in a full production at the Edinburgh Fringe Festival next summer.

September 2018

Oklahoma at St. Ann's Warehouse

Returning to this project after three years, I will be assisting Music Director Nathan Koci and Arranger Daniel Kluger on the Brooklyn remount of the 2015 Bard Summerscape production of Oklahoma, featuring new bluegrass arrangements in a minimal production.

August 2018

Berkshire Opera Festival's Rigoletto

I will be singing in the opera chorus with BOF for the first time on their production of Rigoletto, with performances at Pittsfield's Colonial Theatre.

Summer 2018

The Muny's Centennial Season

During my first time in St. Louis, I worked as a music assistant on seven productions including the first production of Jerome Robbins' Broadway since 1989 and a new orchestration of The Wiz as a part of this impressively historical season at the Muny.

May 2018

New Ithacappella Album!

Ithacappella, the a cappella group I music directed this past year, has fully recorded an eight-track album with  Plaid Productions, to be released this upcoming fall and featuring some of my arrangements and vocals.
